About Us

Welcome to the official online voting platform for the City of Calgary. Our mission is to advance the democratic process through a secure, accessible, and transparent electoral platform that empowers every citizen to participate in shaping the future of our city. At the core of our initiative is the integration of blockchain technology, a pioneering advancement in digital security. 

Through blockchain, each vote cast is meticulously encrypted and permanently recorded, ensuring the utmost integrity and immutability of the electoral process. This robust security framework safeguards your vote from any form of tampering or manipulation, guaranteeing that your voice is authentically represented in the outcome of every election or poll. In our commitment to accessibility, we have eliminated the barriers of time and location that traditionally hinder citizen participation in the electoral process. 

With our online platform, you have the convenience and flexibility to exercise your democratic right to vote from any location with internet connectivity, at any time during the duration of the election or poll. This empowers every member of our community to engage in civic discourse and contribute to the collective decision-making process. Transparency and accountability are fundamental principles guiding our operations. We operate with unwavering transparency at every stage of the voting process, fostering trust and confidence among our constituents. Whether you are participating in a municipal election, a referendum, or any other civic engagement, you can rely on our platform to uphold the highest standards of fairness and accuracy in the tabulation of results. 

We extend our sincere gratitude to you for choosing to participate in the democratic governance of our city through online voting. Your engagement is pivotal in shaping the trajectory of Calgary's future, and together, we can build a more equitable, inclusive, and prosperous community for all residents. 

Thank you for entrusting us with your vote and for joining us on this journey toward a stronger democracy.
