faq

If your inquiry is not addressed within this FAQ section, we encourage you to contact us for further assistance and clarification.

What are the requirements for online voting?

In order to register for an online account. You must fulfill the following requirements,

       1. You must be a Canadian citizen;

       2. You must be 18 years of age or older; and

       3. You must reside in Calgary.

How do I register to vote online?

To create a new account, you can navigate to the "Login" option located in the upper-right corner. Then click "Don't have an account?". Following this step, you will be prompted to answer four inquiries to confirm your eligibility. Upon successful verification of your eligibility status, you will be redirected to the registration page to proceed with creating your account.

Can I change my vote after submitting it online?

No, once you have submitted your vote online, it cannot be changed. We encourage careful consideration before finalizing your vote to ensure it accurately reflects your choice.

What if I encounter technical difficulties while voting online?

We are here to ensure that your voting experience is smooth and hassle-free. If you encounter any technical issues while voting online, you can reach out to us for assistance by calling (+1) 403-555-4444. Our support team is available round-the-clock on election day to help you resolve any problems you may have.

Alternatively, you can also visit the "Contact Us" page on our website to submit your queries electronically. You queries will be processed within 3 working days.

Are online voting results publicly accessible?

Yes, the online voting results are publicly accessible. Once the elections or pollings have concluded, the final results will be released on the result page of our website.

Can I vote online if I'm not a resident of Calgary?

No, online voting is only available to residents of Calgary. In order to participate in online voting, you must be a resident of the city.

Can I vote online if I don't have access to the internet?

No, you cannot vote online without internet access. The system requires internet connectivity to authenticate users. Therefore, individuals without internet access will not be able to participate in online voting.

Are there any security measures in place to prevent fraud or hacking?

Yes. Elections Online Calgary adapt blockchain technology to ensure data integrity and prevent fraud/hacking. Blockchain employs sophisticated cryptographic techniques to encode the information contained within each vote. This encryption ensures that voters' choices remain confidential and cannot be deciphered by unauthorized parties. 

Once a vote is cast and recorded on the blockchain, it becomes part of an immutable and transparent ledger. It is virtually impossible to alter or manipulate the recorded votes without detection. Any attempt to tamper with the records would require consensus from the majority of participants in the blockchain network, making it highly secure against fraudulent activities.
